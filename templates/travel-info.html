Travel

Flights

The airline you're most likely to travel with is Air India. They will do connecting flights to New Delhi or Mumbai and then to Aurangabad

The advice we have received is to directly book with Air India, this way you will be guaranteed a seat on the plan.

If you are travelling from London it will take about a day to arrive in Aurangabad. Therefore, make sure you leave plenty of time to settle in and catch up with yourself! Do you want to start a wedding severely travel exhausted?
